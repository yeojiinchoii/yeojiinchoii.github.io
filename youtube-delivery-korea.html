Intro —

Overview

The YouTube Delivery Korea project of 2021 was a 3D virtual show that helped YouTube connect with its primary partners despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic. The Delivery campaign by YouTube South Korea is an annual event that aims to excite and educate marketers about YouTube products. However, due to months of lockdown, professional marketers wanted a more engaging experience than a standard livestream event. To meet this need, the YouTube Delivery Korea project was launched in 2021, providing a highly immersive and captivating experience for our audience of marketers.

My Role

I was responsible for wireframing, prototyping, and designing the UI, as well as identifying and incorporating UX best practices. The purpose of these efforts was to ensure that the show concept was well-supported and successful.

Understand —

Audience

The YouTube Delivery Korea audience refers to the group of professional marketers who are targeted by the YouTube South Korea Delivery campaign, as well as those who attended the YouTube Delivery Korea project of 2020. This audience is made up of individuals who are interested in learning about and promoting YouTube's products and services, and who are looking for innovative and engaging ways to connect with YouTube and its partners.

Define —

Goals

The following goals were identified for the YouTube Delivery 2021 campaign:

"How might we find innovative ways of approaching consumers in this COVID-19 era?"

Approach —

Virtual 3D Theatre Experience

As part of the design process, the team identified that creating an exclusive and exciting venue was critical to making the platform more appealing to users. Therefore, the team focused on designing a venue that would entice users to explore and interact with YouTube content, ultimately enhancing the overall user experience.

User Flow

I created this onboarding user flow for the team to help achieve the goals of the YouTube Delivery 2021 campaign. Each flow has its own unique content that we discussed to ensure feasibility for implementation or identify opportunities to improve the content for the flow.

Screen Folw

To ensure a seamless user journey, I initially used a dummy stage while the UK team was working on the 3D stage. Once I received the 3D designs, I integrated them into the user journey and updated the background to create a more cohesive experience. I presented this updated user journey to our team, particularly the development team, to provide clear and detailed information for implementation.

Design —

Solution Design

Marketers received a physical ticket with a QR code for the YouTube Delivery 2021 event. The code led them to a microsite featuring a show by Korean influencers. After the show, they accessed a virtual 3D theatre with interviews, clips, and quizzes. New content was added monthly to enhance engagement.

Results —

Business Impact

I can't share specific details of the YouTube Delivery Korea 2021 project results due to confidentiality. But based on measurable indicators, it successfully achieved its goals of connecting YouTube with key partners and providing a more engaging experience for marketers. The project was well-received, likely strengthening YouTube's relationships with partners in South Korea. It also demonstrated YouTube's commitment to innovation and adaptability during the pandemic. We received positive feedback internally and externally, and it was a great opportunity to work with talented individuals on the project.
