Brief —

Background

Bring back the insights pump experience from 2019 and refresh it for Google Marketing Live 2022 events.

Approch —

Design approach

Our brief was to revisit the Insights Pump Experience that was used in the CMO Academy event in 2019 and bring this up to date by aligning with the GML 2022 Brand standards.

We’ve drawn on the use of iconography & bold, striking layouts to refresh the Insights Pump Experience.

We’ve focussed on the Blue & Yellow palette options to ensure we’re feeling distinctly Google. We felt the Red had a tendency to look valentines oriented. We have referenced the recommendations for palette to ensure this works within the GML event context.

3D approach

One of the great surprise & delight elements of the insights pump is seeing a 3D object inflate & then an insight reveal itself when the object floats away.

Our visual approach for the 3D objects will draw on the GML photography style while applying a little more texture consideration to ensure that objects are clearly inflatables.

Colors will draw from the GML brand palettes and consider a tone-on-tone approach.

Design —

Wireframes

In order to inspire our design process, we’ve guessed at possible questions we could be illustrating. We’ve then applied the new visual approach using placeholder 3D objects.

Inflatable objects (Insight 1)

Topic: Connected TV/Online Video
Inflatable options could represent the technology involved with TV or perhaps something related to a specific category of content.

Inflatable objects (Insight 2)

Topic: Innovation / AI / Machine Learning
Here we’ve explored gaming artefacts or more quirky representations of AI and machine learning - a robot or brain.
