Intro —

Background

Digital Guru is an online event platform and marketing website that helps media agencies become experts in marketing with Google. The purpose of the program is to empower the most dedicated and talented individuals in digital marketing to become experts in Google Ads tools.

My Role

As a senior designer on the project team, my responsibilities included contributing to UX design best practices, providing art direction, and establishing the brand design look and feel. Additionally, I conducted user-centred research and design activities to understand user value and develop/improve the project's UX. Through user-centred research and design activities, I improved the UX for the Digital Guru web app that enabled facilitators and audiences to join online events and see their progress. Also, I created UI designs based on the insights and supported the development team in implementing the designs.

Understand —

Audience

Our target audience consists of Google media agency partners in the Asia-Pacific region. We collaborated with Google to develop and enhance their Digital Guru educational program, providing website development and other services. The program helps participants gain valuable insights and skills to succeed in digital marketing with Google, with four modules designed to meet the needs of both facilitators and participants. I designed the website to ensure that it meets the requirements of both target groups.

Define —

Goals

Revamped the web application with a fresh and updated appearance that adhered to the design principles of Google and the unique design requirements of Digital Guru. Also, improved UX for ease of use. Based on user feedback and analytics data from previous web apps in 2020 and 2021, we identified several key pain points that needed to be addressed in our redesign. These included confusing navigation, non-intuitive modules and my progress process, and a lack of mobile responsiveness, among others.

Here were the goals that we aimed to achieve during the redesign:
1. Reorganize and restructure the navigation
2. Categorize the Events Listing page clearly between upcoming and past events
3. Make the Event detail page clearer
4. Enable Live surveys and Live chat without complications during the Live event
5. Display the My Progress page more accessible
6. Align with Google design guidelines and Digital Guru design considerations for a new look & feel

UX Audit

After defining goals, I conducted a UX audit to assess existing web pages. It helped me identify where design falls short and highlight successes. Following this, I ensured the audit evaluated design against goals, enabling me to prioritize improvements where necessary.

User Flow

Before I created wireframes, I developed a user flow that outlined the steps users take to accomplish their goals within the app. This user flow helped identify pain points and opportunities for improvement in the user journey, but also it put the team on the same page.

Design —

Mockup

I used the user flow and goals we previously outlined as a basis for creating mockups that visualized the initial design of the app. Also, once we finalized the art direction, another designer joined the team to work on other pages as there were many pages that needed to be built.

Feedback

To ensure that the design, production, and development teams were aligned, we held regular meetings throughout the project. During one of these meetings, we conducted a usability testing session where participants were able to join a live event and receive a certificate upon completion. Based on the results of the testing, I addressed some of the issues that were found.

Solution Design

I have included some of the solution designs that met our goals. These designs featured clean and simple layouts, easily accessible content, and a mobile-friendly interface.


Results —

Business Impact

The application was successfully launched during the pandemic, and both facilitators and audiences were able to join online courses in real time. The website received positive feedback from Google Ads official team in the USA, and has helped many marketers in their pursuit of Google Ads certification.
