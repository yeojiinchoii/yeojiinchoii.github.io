Intro —

Background

Blunt™ Umbrellas is an exceptional product company in the process of expanding globally. Blunt™ wanted to increase the effectiveness and profitability of their e-commerce website as well as establish advertising systems that could replicate local successes again-and-again throughout new markets.

Understand —

Audience

The YouTube Delivery Korea audience refers to the group of professional marketers who are targeted by the YouTube South Korea Delivery campaign, as well as those who attended the YouTube Delivery Korea project of 2021. This audience is made up of individuals who are interested in learning about and promoting YouTube's products and services, and who are looking for innovative and engaging ways to connect with YouTube and its partners. They may include marketing professionals, advertising executives, social media influencers, and other industry experts who are seeking to expand their knowledge and skills in the field of digital marketing.

Problems

Observing user behaviors via a tool called Inspectlet provides insight into identifying user needs and problems that can be improved. So, I used it to identify problems. One issue was that many users were struggling to see the color changes on the displayed product when a color was selected on mobile, since it was out of the mobile screen size. As a result, they had to scroll up and down to see the color changes.

Additionally, we found another problem through user feedback that users were ordering small-sized umbrellas without noticing the actual size. People tend to avoid pricey items, and this led them to receive a small-sized umbrella.
